This dashboard displays local visitor logs stored in your browser. To collect real-time data from global recruiters and clients on your live website, configure these options:

  • Option A: Google Analytics (GA4)
    Paste your GA4 Measurement ID in js/analytics.js to populate direct dashboard tracking inside your Google Analytics console.
  • Option B: Serverless Database Webhook
    Uncomment the sendBeacon function in js/analytics.js to stream real-time visit JSON records directly to a database catalog (like Supabase, Firebase, or Formspree).
