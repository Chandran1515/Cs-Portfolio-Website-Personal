About

Architecture is the foundation.
Technology is the tool.

I'm Chandran Shanmugam, a licensed architect in India with deep experience in California residential projects — working across Palo Alto, County of Monterey, and Carmel-by-the-Sea with Studio Schicketanz, one of California's respected boutique architecture firms.

My work spans the full project lifecycle: from schematic design and Title 24 consultant coordination to permit coordination and construction documentation under CBC, CRC, and California municipal codes.

As an added value, I develop custom Revit automation plugins (via CS Revit Labs) to accelerate architectural production and eliminate manual workflows for AEC teams. I hold Autodesk Certified Professional (ACP) certifications in both Revit and AutoCAD.
