About Chandran

I am Ar. Chandran Shanmugam, a registered Architect and BIM Developer with 8+ years of total professional experience. My career has focused on bridging physical architecture with software development, including 3.5+ years in California at Studio Schicketanz (Sep 2022 – Apr 2026) where I led BIM automation initiatives, and 3 years at a reputed Australia-based architecture firm in Bangalore, India (2019 – 2022) focusing on sustainable design detailing and automation scripting.

My journey into software engineering began out of necessity. Faced with hours of manual sheet layouts, drafting, and file exporting, I started building C# automations. That evolved into writing custom plugins, integrating LLMs into BIM environments, and studying Computer Science.

I hold a **Master of Architecture (M.Arch)** from Meenakshi College of Engineering, Chennai (Graduated 2019) and a **Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch)** from Tamilnadu School of Architecture, Coimbatore. Today, I build high-performance desktop plugins, WebGL 3D viewers, and custom AI tools in C#/.NET and Python, delivering clean, maintainable codebases that automate architectural workflows at scale.
